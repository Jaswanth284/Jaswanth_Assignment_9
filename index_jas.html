      <h2>About This Website</h2>

      <p>

        This website presents a set of visualizations based on a dataset of

        renewable energy usage, CO₂ emissions, forest area, and power

        consumption for Norway, Spain, Brazil, India, and Egypt from 2010 to

        2024. The charts were created in Google Colab using Altair and then

        exported as images.

      </p>

      <p>

        Each page focuses on a different type of visualization: scatter plots

        for color and size encoding, bar charts for comparison and composition,

        and area/line charts for showing trends. The goal is to explore how

        countries progress toward cleaner energy and how their environmental

        indicators change over time.

      </p>

    </section>
