      <img src="images/jas_bar_stacked.png" alt="Stacked bar chart renewable energy">

      <p>

        The stacked bar chart shows the average share of renewable energy in

        each continent for every year. Each bar represents one year, and the

        colored segments indicate how much each continent contributes to the

        total. This chart reveals that Europe has some of the highest

        renewable percentages, driven mainly by Norway and Spain. South

        America also contributes a significant portion due to Brazil. The

        stacked structure lets me see both the overall increase in renewable

        energy and the regional composition in a single view.
