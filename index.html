About This Website

This website presents a set of visualizations based on a dataset of renewable energy usage, CO₂ emissions, forest area, and power consumption for Norway, Spain, Brazil, India, and Egypt from 2010 to 2024.

The charts were created in Google Colab using Altair and exported as images.
